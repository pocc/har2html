Source repo
  • To Install: Drag and drop this link Empty Notion Trash to your Bookmarks Bar
  • To Use: Click on the bookmark when you are on notion.so
  • Why: Notion used to limit the number of blocks for free users to 1000. Some people want all of their pages in their trash to be permanently deleted even though those files will be deleted automatically after a time. Or maybe it's cathartic to watch files be permanently deleted like an upward waterfall.
